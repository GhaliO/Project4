Deploying Your Website

Congratulations on building your first website! Now, let's make it accessible to the world. Follow these steps to deploy your website:

  1. Choose a Hosting Provider: To make your website available on the internet, select a hosting provider, such as GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel.
  2. GitHub Pages Example: If you choose GitHub as your hosting provider, follow these steps:
    1. Head over to GitHub and sign in if you donâ€™t have an account already.
    2. Upload all your HTML and CSS files to the repository.
    3. Navigate to repository settings where you can choose the branch you want to use for GitHub Pages.
    4. Your website will be accessible at a link similar to this: https://username.github.io/repositoryname.

Remember, the process may vary depending on your chosen hosting provider. Refer to their documentation for detailed instructions.

Now, your website is ready to be shared with the world!
